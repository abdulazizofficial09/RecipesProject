Description

The national dish of Uzbekistan. Fluffy long-grain rice slow-steamed with tender lamb or beef, julienned yellow and orange carrots, and caramelized onions, seasoned with aromatic cumin, whole garlic, and optional chickpeas and raisins for a touch of sweetness

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Oil
  • Meat
  • Onions
  • Carrots

Steps

  1. Prepare the Rice/li>
  2. Fry the Meat and Onions
  3. Caramelize the Carrots
  4. Simmer the Zirvak:
  5. Add the Rice
  6. Absorb and Steam
